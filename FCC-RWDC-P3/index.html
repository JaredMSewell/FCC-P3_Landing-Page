SERVICES

Website Design

As a small business owner you need the best ROI for every dollar you invest in your business. You want and need your website to be on the cutting edge of technology and to be as fast and responsive as possible. In a word, you need your business website to be UNIQUE! Using CMS's like WordPress, or WYSIWYG's, or site builders like WIX will absolutely save you money. But you will end up with a website that is not impressive, not fast, not unique, and in some cases, not owned by you!

Project Management

It is one thing to be able to write some code, direct resources and libraries, and make it all look and function professionally. It is quite another thing to do all of that within a reasonable timeframe while dealing with troubleshooting issues, change orders, complaints, requests, and everything else. Good time management and project management skills and awarenes are absolutely essential to the successful launch of your bussinesses website. A website that must be as unique as you and your business!

Deliverables

I don't just manage your web development project and hack the code to make it work. I keep a detailed accounting of all the time spent on every aspect of it, a copy of every stage of the code used, and every image or illustration created for the project. I maintain this collection of information from the developement of your project because at the end of the project, whether you maintain your working relationship with me or not, you own the work you paid for and you deserve to have a hard copy of all of it.
